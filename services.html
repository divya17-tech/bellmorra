Our Manufacturing Process

We follow a meticulous 5-step process to ensure that every product we manufacture meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and effectiveness.

1

Consultation & Planning

We begin by understanding your brand vision, target market, and specific requirements to create a tailored manufacturing strategy.

2

Research & Development

Our expert chemists develop innovative formulas using premium ingredients, ensuring efficacy and compliance with regulations.

3

Sampling & Approval

We provide product samples for your evaluation and feedback, making necessary adjustments until you're completely satisfied.

4

Production & Quality Control

Once approved, we move to full-scale production with strict quality control measures at every stage of manufacturing.

5

Packaging & Delivery

We handle custom packaging, labeling, and efficient shipping to ensure your products reach you on time and in perfect condition.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our manufacturing services and processes.

Does Bellmora Global provide documented certifications for the products they formulate?

Yes, we provide complete documentation and certifications for all our products, including ISO, FDA, GMP, and other relevant certifications based on your market requirements. All our facilities are regularly audited and certified to meet international standards.

What manufacturing facilities are offered by Bellmora Global?

We offer state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities with fully automated production lines, quality control laboratories, and packaging units to handle everything from small batches to large-scale production. Our facilities include clean rooms, advanced mixing equipment, and automated filling lines.

What are the formulation capabilities of Bellmora Global?

Our R&D team has expertise in creating formulations for skincare, haircare, color cosmetics, and personal care products. We work with natural, organic, and synthetic ingredients to create products that meet your specific requirements. We can develop water-based, anhydrous, emulsion, and serum formulations.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for private label products?

Our MOQ varies by product type and complexity. Generally, we require a minimum order of 1000 units for standard products, but we can be flexible for new brands launching their first products. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How long does the product development process take?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the formulation. Standard products can be developed in 2-4 weeks, while complex custom formulations may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Does packaging design include in contract manufacturing service?

Yes, we offer comprehensive packaging design services as part of our contract manufacturing solutions. Our design team can create custom packaging that reflects your brand identity, or we can work with your existing packaging designs. We also have relationships with packaging suppliers to help you source materials at competitive prices.

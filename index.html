Frequently Asked Questions

Does Bellmora Global provide documented certifications for the products they formulate?

Yes, we provide complete documentation and certifications for all our products, including ISO, FDA, GMP, and other relevant certifications based on your market requirements.

What manufacturing facilities are offered by Bellmora Global?

We offer state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities with fully automated production lines, quality control laboratories, and packaging units to handle everything from small batches to large-scale production.

What are the formulation capabilities of Bellmora Global?

Our R&D team has expertise in creating formulations for skincare, haircare, color cosmetics, and personal care products. We work with natural, organic, and synthetic ingredients to create products that meet your specific requirements.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for private label products?

Our MOQ varies by product type and complexity. Generally, we require a minimum order of 1000 units for standard products, but we can be flexible for new brands launching their first products.

How long does the product development process take?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the formulation. Standard products can be developed in 2-4 weeks, while complex custom formulations may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
